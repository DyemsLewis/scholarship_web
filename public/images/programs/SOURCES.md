# Program Logo Sources

- `dost-logo-card.jpg` is prepared from the DOST seal available through Wikimedia Commons, whose file record identifies the Department of Science and Technology as the source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:DOST_seal.svg
- `ched-logo-card.jpg` is prepared from the official CHED Region III logo download: https://region3.ched.gov.ph/chedlogo.png
- `tulay-aral-logo.png` and `bukas-kinabukasan-logo.png` are original fictional demo-provider marks generated specifically for this project.

The downloaded square logos are centered on a landscape background so existing scholarship cards can display them without cropping the agency marks.
